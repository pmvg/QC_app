STATISTICS


In this place is presented in form of table statistic values regarding variable tested only. This values can be from printed material or calculated by application.
This includes

Number of observations
Sum
Mean
Maximum
Minimum
Median
Standard deviation
Variation
Amplitude


The mean or sum will be used to do test, mean or sum test if printed statistic does exist.


This tables allow searching for year and month for printed and calculated and also permit saving in file those values for calculated.
