GRAPHICS


In here we see some visual representations of data that is been tested.

This includes:

1)	Histogram

 Will show distribution of values over class values.


2)	Time plot

 Will show the distribution of values over time.
 It will have a choice of see more detailed time periods and a table values.


3)	Deviation from mean

 In this one will be calculated a diference between mean and the value. this may allow us to check on spikes or extreme values in data serie.
